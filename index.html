Welcome to College Hostel

The aim of our Hostel is to make it a Home away from Home with focused education. The air-cooled hostel has a pleasant ambiance with the main focus on studies and bringing out latent qualities of the students by promoting sports, cultural activities and interpersonal development. Our hostel provides a comfortable and affordable accommodation for college students. We offer a range of facilities including a gym, library, and common room.

About Our Hostel

Asansol Engineering Hostel is a popular accommodation option for engineering students studying in Asansol. The hostel is known for its modern amenities, comfortable living spaces, and affordable pricing, making it an ideal choice for students seeking a home away from home.

The hostel provides a safe and secure environment for students, with round-the-clock security personnel stationed at the premises. It also offers a range of facilities such as a common room, study area, mess hall, and recreational facilities to ensure that students can enjoy a well-rounded experience.
